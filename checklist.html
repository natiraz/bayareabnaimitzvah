Tap an item to check it off. Everything you need for a beautiful ceremony.

✨ We joyfully provide

Included for your ceremony

  • Gorgeous handwritten Torah and decorative velvet Torah cover
  • Torah Yad (pointer)
  • 24 light-blue silk kippot (optional)
  • 1 ritual table (36-inch high-top round)
  • 1 ceramic Kiddush cup
  • Siddurim (prayer books) — we design and print them; your family covers the printing costs
  • 6 white coiled-rope candy baskets
  • Silk Jerusalem handmade challah cover and challah platter
  • Extra handmade tallit for Torah blessings
  • Printed writing pieces (Torah portion, Torah speech, student welcoming, etc.)

🌱 Family provides

A few things to gather

  • Student tallit (prayer shawl)
  • Tables for the Torah Ark and reading, plus tablecloths (venue or family usually provides)
  • Student workbook
  • 3–4 challahs for the ceremony
  • Wine or grape juice
  • Sunkist Fruit Gems candy for candy-throwing (2–3 bags)
  • PA system with three cabled microphones on stands

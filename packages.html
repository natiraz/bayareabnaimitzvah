רוח

Ruach Package

Spirit · 9 months

Weekly 45-minute meetings for 9 months, plus three family meetings. Includes a personalized binder and our Bay Area B'nai Mitzvah Siddur. Miriam co-creates the service with your family, ensures your child knows the correct melodies, and creates personalized audio recordings for the prayers and Torah portion. She works one-on-one on the D'var Torah and the deeper meaning of the service, and—as Founder—officiates the ceremony. A two-hour rehearsal is provided two weeks prior.

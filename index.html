Our Philosophy

The Bar, Bat, and B' Mitzvah ritual is a sacred time for both the family, the child, as well as the teacher. We have designed our work to create a one-on-one mentoring relationship in which we connect with your child to discover their passions and infuse these passions into our lessons. We believe that in order for your child to feel successful in the Bar/Bat/B' Mitzvah process, they need to feel empowered, motivated, and connected to the deeper spiritual purpose of this meaningful rite of passage.

We gently guide your child to connecting deeper with Judaism, prayer, and their relationship to the beautiful ceremony that we will co-create with your family. We see the Bat/Bar/B' Mitzvah as a step in the life-long journey of the Jewish path, and want to help pave the way for a deep authentic connection that lasts a lifetime.

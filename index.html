חַיִּים · Our Philosophy

A sacred time, held with joy.

The Bar, Bat, and B' Mitzvah ritual is a sacred time for the family, the child, and the teacher alike. We've designed our work as a one-on-one mentoring relationship — connecting with your child to discover their passions and weave them into every lesson.

We gently guide your child toward a deeper connection with Judaism, with prayer, and with the beautiful ceremony we will co-create alongside your family.

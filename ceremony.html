Our Approach

A ceremony that is truly yours

Co-created ceremonies designed with love

Customized B'nai Mitzvah Rituals

Our goal is to help your family create a ceremony that is meaningful as well as a beautiful expression of your child's special day. We support you in all aspects of designing a meaningful ceremony that feels personal.

Jewish tradition with a renewed vision

Meaningful and Relevant Judaism

We aspire to honor the Jewish tradition, while making it relevant to the modern day Bay Area Jewish Bar/Bat Mitzvah student. We have supported families coming from a variety of religious backgrounds, as well as interfaith and LGBTQ families.

Support in creating a beautiful Torah speech

Ongoing Torah Drash Support

We support our B'nai Mitzvah students to create a Torah Speech that is dynamic and interesting and a reflection of their interpretation of their Torah portion. This is done through personalized attention and many lessons are dedicated to the writing of the Torah speech, which is truly the student's moment to shine.
